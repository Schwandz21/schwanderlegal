How It Works

Simple, file-by-file coverage.

The goal is to make coverage easy to request, easy to scope, and easy to complete.

Send the request.

Share the county/location, preferred signing time, file type, package status, printing requirements, scanback needs, and deadline.

Confirm scope and availability.

Schwander Legal confirms whether the file fits the limited coverage model, whether the timing works, and what fee applies based on travel, urgency, and logistics.

Execute the appointment.

The package is reviewed for execution logistics, the appointment is handled professionally, signatures are completed, and required scanbacks or package return steps are performed.

Close the loop.

Your office receives completion confirmation, notes regarding any issues, and returned documents according to your file instructions.

Compliance-Focused Scope

Not witness-only. Not a title company. Attorney coverage.

Schwander Legal is built around a focused, limited-scope model for firms that already maintain compliant file, title, escrow, and settlement processes.

Initial service model:

Limited-scope mobile closing coverage and document execution support for files assigned by Delaware real estate law firms, settlement attorneys, and title-law practices.

Unless expressly agreed in writing, Schwander Legal does not open title, issue title insurance, hold settlement funds, disburse funds, record documents, clear title defects, or serve as primary settlement counsel.

The purpose is to support compliant files — not to replace the assigning firm’s role, settlement process, or professional responsibility.
