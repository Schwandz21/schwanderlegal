How It Works

Simple, fast, file-by-file coverage.

The process is structured to be easy to request, easy to scope, and easy to complete.

2

3

Handle the appointment

4

Close the loop

Your office receives completion confirmation, issue notes if any, and returned package workflow.

Initial service model:

Limited-scope mobile closing coverage and document execution support for files assigned by Delaware real estate law firms, settlement attorneys, and title-law practices.

Unless expressly agreed in writing, Schwander Legal does not open title, issue title insurance, hold settlement funds, disburse funds, record documents, clear title defects, or serve as primary settlement counsel.

The goal is to support compliant files — not replace the assigning firm’s file ownership, title work, escrow process, or professional responsibility.

Scope

Attorney coverage, not title-company cosplay.

This is a focused service for firms that already control the file and need reliable attorney support at the execution stage.
